Making art has always been my number one hobby, and my biggest personality trait. I see the art in the big and little parts of life. Every conversation is art to me. Everything around me is art.
I love to paint. My room is covered in my works of art, to a point where there is no longer space on the walls. I'm too scared to sell my work because I am so attached to my pieces.
Crocheting is a new form of art that I have recently picked up. I watched tutorial videos on the basics of learning and have taught myself new patterns to create fun things like hats and sweaters.
